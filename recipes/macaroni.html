Southern-Style Baked Macaroni & Cheese

Grandma's southern-style baked mac & cheese

Prep time: 30 minutes. Cook time: 35 minutes

Servings: 10.

per serving: If you are counting calories, this recipe is NOT for you.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups elbow macaroni noodles
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or sour cream
  • 8 oz Wisconsin cheddar smoked beachwood cheese
  • 10 oz hoop cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Colby & Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup shredded milk cheddar cheese
  • 2 eggs

Ingredients

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 9X9 casserole dish.
  2. Boil macaroni for 8 minutes.
  3. Shred cheese while waiting for noodles.
  4. In large boil, combine eggs, milk, cream, all cheese except the Wisconsin cheddar.Mix well.
  5. Place one thin layer of cheese mix in dish.
  6. Layer of macaroni noodles.
  7. Another layer of cheese mix.
  8. Repeat until all product is used up.
  9. Top with Wisconsin cheese
  10. Bake for 35 minutes.
