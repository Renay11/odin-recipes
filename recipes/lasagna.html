Anabolic Vegetarian Lasagna

Protein-packed, low-fat vegetarian lasagna with lots of vegetables.

Prep Time: 20 minutes. Cook time: 40 minutes

Servings: 6. Calories per serving: 293

Ingredients

  • 2 sliced zucchinis
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cups frozen spinach
  • 8 oz mushrooms
  • 16 oz fat-free cottage cheese
  • 7 oz Mozzarella fat-free shredded cheese
  • 24 oz pasta sauce
  • 1 bag Morning Star Chorizo veggie crumbles

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 9X13 casserole dish.
  2. Saute onions and mushrooms in skillit.
  3. Add sauce and chorizo to mix, let simmer.
  4. Drain spinach, then combine with cottage cheese, mix well.
  5. Place thin layer of sauce mixture to bottome of casserole dish.
  6. Next, place one layer of zucchinis
  7. A layer of the spinach mixture.
  8. Add a layer of sauce mix and top with thin layer of shredded cheese
  9. Repeat until everythin is all used.
  10. Top with the remaining shredded cheese
  11. Bake for 35 minutes
