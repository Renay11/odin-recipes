Anabolic Vegetarian Lasagna

Protein-packed, low-fat vegetarian lasagna with lots of fibrous vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 zucchinis
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 8 oz mushrooms
  • 16 oz fat-free cottage cheese
  • 7 oz Mozzarella fat-free shredded cheese
  • 24 oz pasta sauce
  • 1 bag Morning Star Chorizo veggie crumbles
