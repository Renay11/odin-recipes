Anabolic French Toast

Protein-packed french toast

3 minutes 34 minutes

5. 295

Ingredients

  • Sara Lee Delightful 45 bread(15 slices), or other low calorie multigrain bread
  • 32 oz liquid egg whites
  • Truvia(about 7 packets) or other sugar substitute
  • 1/4 cup vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons of cinnamon powder
  • sugar-free syrup
  • raspberries or your favorite fruit

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Ready large cookie sheet with parchment paper and pam.
  3. Combine egg white, sugar substitute, cinnamon and vanilla extract in large bowl and whisk until throughly blended.
  4. Soak bread slices in egg mixture until saturated.
  5. Place soaked bread on cookie sheet.
  6. Bake for 17 minutes then turn bread on other side and bake for another 17 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and top with syrup and fruit
